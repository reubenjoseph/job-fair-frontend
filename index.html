About Us

IEEE job fair is a joint initiative taken up by the IEEE Kochi section and IEEE Kochi subsection.This is the third consecutive year that the fair is being conducted.IEEE Job Fair 2018 is the perfect platform for recruiters and the candidates to interact and discover their common ground.The candidates get to show their true potential and stand a chance to work with experts from various fields.With the wide array of companies arriving at the fair, the candidates get more exposure and choices.IEEE job fair is also the ideal place for companies to hire employees who fit the bill.It is one of the few places where candidates are chosen based on their calibre and knowledge rather than being evaluated on the basis of their grades.With the huge successes of the previous two job fairs and having won the IEEE award for best event conducted by a Student Branch across the world, this year’s job fair promises to deliver and live up to its reputation.

About IEEE

IEEE is the world’s renowned professional organisation for engineers, catering to over 100,000 members worldwide. IEEE Kerala Section is one of the most active Section, having won the Outstanding Large Section Award from IEEE HQ for the year 2015. The vibrant student community along with the passionate Young Professionals make Kerala Section one of the most active in the world, conducting a variety of events and activities.
